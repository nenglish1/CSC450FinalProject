Welcome to Online Risk!

Please read the instructions thoroughly before beginning a game.

Select "Easy" or "Hard" to start.

Instructions:

  1. Choose a difficulty. This will link to a new page.
  2. Click "Start Game" to begin.
  3. Deploy troops in your territories.
  4. Select "Change Mode" to enter Attack Mode.
  5. Enter an attack by clicking one of your territories and a bordering enemy territory.
  6. Click "Next Turn" to proceed.
  7. Eliminate the other player to win.
